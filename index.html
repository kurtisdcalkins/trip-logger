Day 1

Saran Wrap

27 June 2020

Saran Wrap

Dad: FZJ-80

Went down to Colorado Springs for the day. Trail was about an hours-drive from the city on dirt roads trhough some mountains. There were a lot of shooters along the way. Made it to the trail about 10am. Aired down to about 10psi. Started up the trail and found the beginning to be easy-to-moderate. The hill climb is steep and rocky. There was some mud which made it much more difficult. Finished the trail around 2pm.

Day 1: Steel Bender Loop

21 October 2020

Steel Bender Loop

Dad: FZJ-80

Warm, but not too hot. Stopped for lunch at a big steep rock face. Played with the RC Crawler for a little while. The loop was fun, but the trail gets a little boring towards the end of the loop. We did not attempt the bick rock ledge.
